PROJECT GREEN MAT

ABOUT

Project Green Mat is an initiative by four students of DPS Ruby Park Kolkata to ensure the growth of crops even in areas with fallow land and undesirable climates. With a rise in global warming due to pollution, along with an increasing requirement of fodder due to a growing population, producing more crops sustainably on a large scale is the need of the hour. With Green Mat, that is now possible. To learn more, click on 'How it works'
